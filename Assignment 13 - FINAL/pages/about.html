About

Reversed Rescue is a registered 501 (c)(3) Non Profit in Los Angeles, CA. Since October, of 2015 RR has saved, rehabilitated and rehomed 46 Pitbull Breed Dogs in Los Angeles that were victims of Fighting, Neglect or Abandonment.

We are called Reversed Rescue; A rescue facility not just focused on rescuing the Pitbull breeds but also about rehabilitating them to be ready to find a forever home. We take on Fight/Bait dogs and severe medical cases along with stray pick-ups all over LA. We are a full training sanctuary for animals and their owners as well, new or old. A facility focused on the animal and the human, hand in hand (Paw). We empower owners with knowledge and focused skill so they can not only have a great pet companion but be a positive voice for the Pitbull breeds under BSL (Breed Specific Legislation) that are flooding shelters and being killed daily.
